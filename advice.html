Signs of Unhealthy Relationships

Your Partner...

1. is threatening, they say "If you dont...I'll......"

2. won't take no for an answer

3. has temper outburst

4. makes you feel scared and afraid that they might hurt you

5. mocks you

6. downgrades how you feel about your self

7. has a possessive behavior, feeling unhappy when you spent time with your family and friends

8. is jealous all the time and accuses you for trying to attract others by the way you dress/walk/behave

9. says "I love you too much, and I can't stand you being with other people."

10. transpass through places in order to find you

11. gives you a sense of being trapped in this relationship

12. There is physical violence.
